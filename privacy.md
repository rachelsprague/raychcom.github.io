# Privacy Policy – Raych

Effective Date: March 2026

Raych (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit [https://raych.com](https://raych.com).

## Information We Collect
- **Usage Data:** We use Google Analytics to track site traffic and user interactions. This data is anonymized and cannot personally identify you.
- **Cookies:** We may use cookies for analytics purposes.

## How We Use Your Information
- To understand how visitors interact with our site and improve user experience.
- No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
- Google Analytics is used to monitor site traffic. You can opt out at [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Your Choices
- You may disable cookies in your browser settings.
- Analytics data is anonymized; no personally identifiable information is collected.
